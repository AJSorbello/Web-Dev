Taco

Back to Recipes List

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • Salsa or hot sauce to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or chicken over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning to the meat according to the packet instructions. Stir until well combined.
  3. Warm the taco shells or tortillas in a separate pan or microwave.
  4. Fill each taco shell with the seasoned meat.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, cheese, diced tomatoes, sour cream, and salsa or hot sauce as desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your tacos!

Difficulty: Easy
